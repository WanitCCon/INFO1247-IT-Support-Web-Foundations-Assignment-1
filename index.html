Martin Luther King Jr.

Biography

Martin Luther King Jr. was a prominent American Civil Rights Movement leader during the 1950s and 1960s. Known for his advocacy of nonviolent protest and his stirring oratory, he championed equality and justice, leading impactful campaigns that contributed to significant legislative changes and societal advancements in the fight against racial segregation and discrimination in the United States.

Famous Quote

I have a dream that one day this nation will rise up and live out the true meaning of its creed.
